These packages typically include accommodation, transportation, and guided tours to explore the city’s highlights, such as the Panfilov Park, Zenkov Cathedral, and the Central State Museum. Visitors can also venture into the surrounding natural beauty, including the nearby mountains for skiing or hiking, and visit attractions like the Shymbulak Ski Resort and Big Almaty Lake. Additionally, tours may include cultural experiences like visiting local markets, sampling Kazakh cuisine, and attending traditional performances. With its blend of urban sophistication and natural splendor, Almaty tour packages promise an unforgettable journey through this dynamic Central Asian city.
